How It Works

Three steps.
From field to register.

Step 01
Register in the field

Personnel complete the 4-step form from anywhere — a polling station, a community centre, a doorstep. Works fully offline; syncs the moment connectivity returns.

Step 02
Review and decide

Higher Authority sees every pending submission in a clean queue. Approve or reject with a required reason — every decision is time-stamped and attributed.

Step 03
Manage and export

Admin manages the team and sees the full register. Export CSV or PDF at any time. The audit log captures every action for accountability.
