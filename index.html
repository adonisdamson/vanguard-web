What It Does

Everything the constituency needs,
nothing it doesn't.

Purpose-built for NDC's registration process — not adapted from a generic form builder.

Field Registration

4-step guided form captures every member detail — name, DOB, polling station, photo — even with no signal.

Review Queue

Higher Authority reviews pending submissions, approves or rejects with reason, and the record updates instantly.

Member Directory

Live search and filter across 100,000+ records by name, polling station, status, or constituency — server-side fast.

Audit Trail

Every action — registration, approval, account change — is logged with actor, timestamp, and full metadata.

Personnel complete the 4-step form from anywhere — a polling station, a community centre, a doorstep. The record queues locally when offline and syncs automatically the moment connectivity returns. Nothing is lost.
